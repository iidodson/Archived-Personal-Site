Notable is a mobile app that lets students take quick notes during class or for anything else. I lead the user research and interface design, which resulted in a prototype for my Foundations of Human Computer Interactions class.

View Prototype

Personas

Through interviewing 20 students about their experiences and expectations of note taking apps, we created these personas.

Requirements Definition

1. Traditional word processing functionality (bolding, autocorrect, fonts, color etc)
2. Easy to use (user friendly)
3. Review section to view content that the user still needs to work on
4. Organization with folders

High Fidelity Prototypes

Analysis

We received on the prototype from our target users. I analyzed their responses based on frequency and sentiment to understand where improvements could be made.
